Holocaust Education: The Legacy of Chiune Sugihara

Who Was Chiune Sugihara?

Chiune Sugihara was a Japanese diplomat who defied orders to issue transit visas to Jewish refugees during World War II, saving thousands of lives.

How Did He Help?

In 1940, Sugihara, as the Japanese Vice-Consul in Lithuania, issued thousands of visas, allowing Jews to escape the Holocaust through Japan. He risked his career and safety to save lives.
